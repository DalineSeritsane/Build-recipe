Carrot Cake


Here are the recipes on how to make your own tasty carrot cake


Ingredients


  • 6 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple , drained
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of backing soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
