Carrot Cake



In this recipe you will learn how to make your own tasty home-made carrot cake.


Ingredients

  • 6 cups grated carrots
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup raisins
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup crushed pineapple , drained
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of backing soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
Instructions

In a medium bowl, combine grated carrots and brown sugar. Set aside for 60 mintues, then stir in raisins.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees). Grease and flour two 10 inch cake pans.

In a large bowl, beat eggs until light. Gradually beat in the white sugar, oil and vanilla. Stir in the pineapples. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon, stir into the wet mixture until absorbed.

Finally stir in the carrot mixture and the walnuts. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

Bake for 45 to 50 minutes in the preheated oven, until cake tests done. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan.

When completely cooled, frost with cream cheese frosting and Enjoy the tasty mouth- melting carrot cake
